WELCOME TO CHATTERBOX

Your 24X7 chatting partner

  • This is a chatroom service
  • You can chat with users who are there in the same room as you are
  • You can change room with the command "/join roomname" where roomname can be any roomname of your choice
  • You can also change your nickname using the command "/nick newname" where newname can be any nickname of your choice
  • To change room you can also click on the room you want to join on the room list given on the side
Chat commands:
  • Change nickname: /nick [username]
  • Join/create room: /join [room name]
